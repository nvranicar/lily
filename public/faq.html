FAQs

What payment methods do you accept?

Pay Pal is the method of payment we accept for those purchasing E-Design services.

Do you offer refunds?

Because of the nature of the product being a pdf file via email, there are no refunds. Depending on which package you choose you may have a few days to request a vision board change and time during the design phase to tweak some of the design elements.

How do I get in touch with you?

You may email us at any time and we will get back with you in a timely manner.

​Do you work with local clients in person?

Yes, if you are located in and near Lexington, KY. we would be glad to work with you in a more traditional ​ capacity. We charge an hourly fee for local design services. Contact us and we will get with you on pricing.

Is E-Design right for me?

E-Design is the perfect way to achieve a professionally designed space with out consuming too much of your ​ time and budget. It is a way for you to implement a design suggestion at a do-it-yourself pace.
