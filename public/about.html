Meet the duo

LI & LY Interiors (pronounced Lee and Lee) is an interior design company based out of Lexington, KY. The owners Lisa (LI) Razor and Kelly (LY) Reynolds are a design duo with 40 plus years’ experience in residential and commercial design. We are dedicated to helping you create inspiring environments by meeting your needs and desires all within a budget. With a fun, creative, personal approach your space will come to life! A well thought out happy space.

The design duo met while working on a church renovation project and had a connection right away. After having their own careers working for various design companies and on their own, they realized they would have a lot of fun expanding their love for good design and desire to help others by creating ​LI & LY Interiors, an on-line (E-Design) company. Let them make your dream room a reality!
