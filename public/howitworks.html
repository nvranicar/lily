How It Works

â€‹E-Design is a simple, virtual design option to create a custom room to meet your personal style, and budget. Clients have the opportunity to obtain a professionally designed space while communicating solely via E-mail.

  1. Choose your package - Contact us and comment which package you are interested in and we will send you a questionnaire via e-mail.
  2. Questionnaire - Complete our design questionnaire to establish budget and room requirements.
  3. Photos & Measurements - Attach to the questionnaire photos & measurements of the Room[s]. Include photos and measurements of existing furniture.
  4. Inspiration - Send us inspirational images, online links, or invite us to join your Pinterest board.
  5. Payment - A Paypal invoice will be sent via E-mail & the design process will begin once payment is paid in full.
  6. Within 2-3 weeks you will receive your vision board and other design elements depending on the package you ordered.
  7. Now it's your turn...Once you receive your E-Design it's time to go shopping and create your new space!
